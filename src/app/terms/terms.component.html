How it works
This service provides the seller and buyer a platform to sell, shop, and interact when they join commercial groups. A commercial group is made up of buyers and sellers who are interested in a certain commercial category, for example Men or Women Apparel. Sellers post an item they want to sell within the network at a discounted rate, and in return they are allowed to post Ads for free. To ensure that sellers with the best prices get the most Ad exposure, we allow consumer users to rate the deals by liking or not liking them. The items for sale with the most likes gets more Ad exposure than items with less likes. If an item reaches a certain threshold of dislikes, the free Ad previlige granted to that seller for the item is temporarily suspended until the seller increases the discount percentage.
