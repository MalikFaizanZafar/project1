How do we help both shoppers and sellers to save$$

Through our “Free Ad for a Discount” ecommerce and marketing model. In this model sellers get to sell their discounted products or services at NO COST to target consumers, and in return the seller get to post FREE Ads to those consumers. But the consumers determine how much exposure the FREE Ad gets by liking or not liking the discount. More likes means more Ad exposure, and less likes means less exposure. If the percentage of likes drops to a certain threshold then the free Ad privilege is temporarily suspended until seller increases discount.
