How it works

This service provides the seller and buyer a platform to sell, shop, and interact.

Sellers post an item they want to sell within the network at a discounted rate, and in return they are allowed to post a FREE Ad for each item they list.

Shoppers get to LIKE or NOT LIKE the deals. More likes means more Ad exposure for the seller, and less likes means less Ad exposure.

If a deal gets a certain threshold of dislikes, the free Ad linked to it is temporarily suspended until the seller increases disount on the deal.
