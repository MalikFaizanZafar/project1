About the PackAd Network


What is the PackAd network?

The PackAd network is a commercial network platform built on an ecommerce and marketing model we call “Free Ad for a Discount” In this platform where businesses gets to list their discounted products or services at no cost to them, and in return the business gets to post a free Ad for each item they list. Consumers in the network determine the amount of exposure the Ads gets by liking or not liking the deals. More likes means more Ad exposure, and less likes means less exposure. If the percentage of likes drops to a certain threshold then the free Ad associated with the deal is suspended until seller boosts discount.

For consumers the network enables you to shop, get great prices, interact and meet other consumers, and possibly make new friends. For businesses the network enables you to sell your products or services at no cost, interact with consumers, and cut your marketing costs by running Ads for free...yes, not a typo, it’s FREE and will always be FREE!

Does PackAd the company sell anything?

No PackAd the company does not sell anything. We just provide the platform for businesses to sell their products or services.

What is PackAd’s dynamic networking model and how does it differ from Social networks?

Unlike social and professional networks like Facebook, Twitter and Linkedin where you interact with a static group of people in your network, the PackAd network is dynamic and different. Dynamic in the sense that the people you interact with at a given time can change depending on your commercial preferences and activities. This model is designed to emulate consumer’s everyday commercial experience. For instance when a consumer goes to a restaurant they can meet and interact with other consumers in that restaurant and potentially make new friends, but when they leave that restaurant and go to maybe a supermarket their network changes from the restaurant people to now the consumers in the supermarket where they can meet, interact and potentially make new friends as well. In essence, consumers connect to and disconnect from other consumers in their ever changing daily commercial experiences. This changing real life dynamic commercial networking model based on a consumer’s commercial activities is what the PackAd network provides.

What can I use the PackAd network for?

The PackAd network is built to be the ultimate platform for ecommerce interaction between consumers and consumers, and consumers and businesses. As a consumer you can use the network to meet and interact with other consumers with similar commercial interest or need and potentially make new friends, interact with businesses selling their products or services in the network, and of course shop for good or services at greatly discounted prices. As a business you can use the network to meet and interact with buyers and potential buyers of your products or services, sell your goods or services, and of course run Ads for free..yes you heard it correctly, run Ads for FREE.

How is the PackAd network different from traditional ecommerce sites?

Traditional ecommerce sites presents platform for consumers to strictly buy things. PackAd network on the other hand provides a platform for consumers to engage with other consumers and businesses from the time they have an idea about buying something to long after they have bought it.

What are commercial groups?

It is how we group consumers and businesses who are interested in a particular product or service category for interactions, shopping, selling and marketing. For example a consumer user who is interested in buying a car and a business user who sells a car will join a commercial group dedicated for auto sale.

Advertising on PackAd network

Is it free to run Ads on the PackAd network?

YES, and will always be FREE. Our business model is not to make money from Ads.

How do I post Ads?

You will have to post a discounted item for sale first in other to be able to post free Ads on our network.

How long does my Ads run for?

You essentially determine how long your Ad runs. An Ad is tied to a sale item so the expiry date you set for the discount on that sale item is also the expiry date of the Ad. So when the sale item discount expires so does the Ad.

Who sees my Ads?

Your Ad is targeted to a specific group of interested consumers. How does the consumers indicate interest? Consumers indicate interest by joining commercial groups for specific category of items.

How often is my Ads displayed?

Consumers primarily control how often your Ad is displayed by liking or not liking your discount. The more likes your discount get the better your display rating which determines how often your Ad is displayed. So the better discount you give that consumers like, the better display you will get.

Can I post multiple Ads at a time?

Yes you can if you list multiple items for sale.
